Planning the research


To find out the problem and  increase VeeR's usability, I worked as a consultant to lead a usability testing to identify usability problems and find out opportunities to help VeeR's localization in US market.

Research Objective

To test VeeR VR Mobile's usability in the following aspects:

Learnability


The time and effort users require to achieve effectiveness of a given task.

Ease Of Use


The time users spent in completing tasks; and the mistakes made by users

Effectiveness


The rate of success users achieve in completing certain tasks.

Attitude


The positive or negative feelings of users toward the system.

Recruiting Participants

I worked with my team and recruited 9 participants who are among VeeR's targeted users for this testing. We found these participants in several apartment buildings' amenity spaces.

Research insights

Interaction Usability Problems

1. Users don't know how to switch to VR mode

Exploration Task 1:
Switch between Mobile/ VR Mode to Watch a Video

This task was given to users when they already navigated through the onboarding, homepage and "for you" video lists, and picked one video to watch.
We kept silence while observing the participants for the first five minutes. Most participants did not even realize there was a VR mode.
We gave prompts to participants in the fifth minute if they didn't complete the task yet: "Try to pause the video, do you find any unfamiliar buttons in the interface? Try to click on it."

Flow of switching from mobile mode to VR mode

User Feedback

“ I thought there was only a mobile mode! It's so hard to notice the VR mode button.”
“ This task is hard, and I felt very overwhelmed not knowing how I should interact with the app”

2. Disrupted Video Watching Experience

From the results of the follow-up questionnaire, 6/9 users expressed they feel the process of docking the phone into the headset interrupted the video watching experience. While they were setting up the headset, the video was still playing, as a result part of the video would be missed.

While user setting up the headset, video was playing

User Feedback

“It made me feel very nervous and I was afraid I did it too slowly.”
