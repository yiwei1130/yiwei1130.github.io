Overview

Project Brief

I led the design for the new search feature for Banmi App to reduce the bounce rate of Banmi's content. The new search featured:

  • Seamless query by showing trend and search history.
  • Scannable and informative results with filters, tabs, and preview.

Solution Overview

Improve the efficiency of content discovery. Easier experience to drill down to the needed content in shortest time.

Background

About Banmi

Banmi Pregnancy School is the most trusted pregnancy and parenting mobile product in China. Their mission is to provide the audience with expert, authoritative, trustworthy and evidence-based health information through animated videos and articles. It is a easily accessed educational information platform that provides support and answers questions whenever needed.
